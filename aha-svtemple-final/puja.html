Puja & Sevas

Sponsor

Participate in daily and special pujas at the temple. Contact us to schedule your seva or for priest services. Our temple priest, Pt. Sudhakar Sharma, conducts all pujas with proper Vedic rituals.

Abhishekam

Monthly Abhishekams

Sponsor abhishekams for Shiva, Vishnu and Parivar deities. Monthly abhishekams are performed with sacred waters, milk, honey, and other sacred items while chanting vedic mantras.

Archana

Daily/Weekly Archana

Offer archana for special days, birthdays and anniversaries. Archanas can be performed in person or on your behalf with 108 or 1008 names of the deity.

Priest Services

Private Puja / Weddings

Priest services at the temple or your home for samskaras, homams, grihapravesham, weddings, etc. Contact us to discuss your requirements and schedule.

Special Puja Sponsorships

Festival Puja

Maha Shivarathri Sponsorship

Sponsor the all-night Maha Shivarathri celebration including Shiva Parvathi Kalyanam. Multiple kala pujas, special abhishekams, and elaborate decorations.
