Festivals & Events

Join us in celebrating major Hindu festivals and cultural programs throughout the year. All community members are welcome to participate.

Mahashivaratri

Allâ€‘Night Puja

Multiple kala pujas, bhajans and darshan through the night. Experience the sacred nightlong vigil with special prayers and offerings to Lord Shiva.

Janmashtami

Krishna Utsav

Special alankaram, bhajans and kids' activities. Celebrate the birth of Lord Krishna with midnight celebrations, devotional songs, and swinging of the cradle.

Navaratri

Devi Celebrations

Daily evening pujas, Lalitha sahasranamam, and cultural programs. Nine nights dedicated to the Divine Mother with special decorations, dandiya raas, and garba.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Lord Ganesha Festival

Special pujas and offerings to Lord Ganesha. Cultural programs, prasadam distribution, and community celebrations.

Diwali

Festival of Lights

Lakshmi puja, diyas lighting, and community gathering. Celebrate the victory of light over darkness with prayers, sweets, and festivities.

Community Programs

Cultural Classes & Events

Regular yoga classes, Sanskrit learning, classical music and dance workshops for children and adults. Check with the temple for current schedule.
