Visitor Guidelines

Please observe these customs when visiting

Dress Code

Modest clothing requested. Traditional Indian attire welcome. Shoulders and knees should be covered.

Remove Footwear

Please remove shoes before entering sanctum areas. Shoe racks provided at entrance.

Photography

Photography allowed in common areas. Please ask permission before photographing during ceremonies.

Silence & Respect

Maintain peaceful atmosphere. Silence mobile phones. Respect worshippers during prayers.

Prasadam

Free blessed food (prasadam) distributed after major pujas. All are welcome to partake.

Donations Welcome

Voluntary donations accepted at temple office or donation boxes. All contributions appreciated.
