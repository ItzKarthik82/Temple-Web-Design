Why Support AHA Temple?

Your generous donations enable us to maintain daily worship, conduct festivals, support educational programs, and serve the community. Every contribution, large or small, makes a meaningful difference.

The temple operates primarily through the support of community members. Your dana (giving) is an act of seva (service) that sustains this sacred space for generations to come.
