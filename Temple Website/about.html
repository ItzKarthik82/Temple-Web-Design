Our Mission

The American Hindu Association (AHA) Shiva Vishnu Temple is devoted to preserving the rich spiritual traditions of Hinduism while fostering a welcoming community for worship, education, and cultural celebration.

We serve as a sacred space where devotees can practice daily prayers, participate in festivals, learn about Hindu philosophy, and connect with fellow community members.

Our History

Est

Founded by Devotees

The AHA Temple was established by a dedicated group of Hindu families seeking to create a spiritual home for worship and cultural preservation in the community.

Our Deities

Sacred sanctums dedicated to divine worship

Lord Shiva

Daily abhishekam, Rudram chanting, and Maha Shivaratri celebrations honor the cosmic transformer and supreme consciousness.

Lord Vishnu

Traditional archana, Vishnu Sahasranama recitation, and seasonal festivals celebrate the preserver and protector.

Goddess Devi

Navaratri festivities, weekly pujas, and special ceremonies invoke divine feminine energy, strength, and compassion.

Additional Deities

Our temple also honors Ganesha, Hanuman, and other deities through daily worship and special occasions.

Community Programs

AHA Gurukulam

Educational programs teaching Hindu philosophy, Sanskrit, cultural arts, and values to youth.

Yoga Classes

Regular yoga sessions promoting physical health, mental peace, and spiritual wellness.

Cultural Events

Music, dance, drama, and art programs celebrating Indian heritage and traditions.

Community Service

Volunteer opportunities, food drives, and outreach programs serving the broader community.
